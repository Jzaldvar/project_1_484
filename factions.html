Factions of Warhammer 40K

Welcome to the galaxy sized food fight. Below is your fast guide to who is who, why they fight, and what vibe they bring to the party.

Fun fact box

Orks believe red paint makes things go faster. In this setting belief has power, so yes red rides go fast. Science cries. Orks laugh.

The Imperium of Man

The biggest human empire. It runs on faith, paperwork, and a very tired golden skeleton on a chair. If it moves, it needs a stamp. If it breathes, it needs a prayer.

  • Astra Militarum: They loves tanks and their troops are cannon fodder.
  • Adeptus Mechanicus: Tech priests who love machines more than friends.
  • Adeptus Custodes: The best of the best and the Big E bodyguards who decided looking like bananas was the look.
  • Adepta Sororitas: Nuns that love flame throwers only second to the Big E.
  • Inquisition: Pretty much corrupt leaders who burn if you dont agree with them.

Loyalist Space Marines

Gene boosted poster boys of humanity. Here are the headliners.

Major Chapters

  • Ultramarines: Games Workshops favorite faction and they also known as "blueberries"
  • Blood Angels: Angel boys who sometimes lose control and kill everyone including friendlys.
  • Dark Angels: Space Knights who take everything too serious.
  • Space Wolves: Pretty much space vikings, they love to drink and fight.
  • Salamanders: The only good guys in the whole universe.
  • Imperial Fists: They love to build walls that no one could breach.
  • Raven Guard: Stealth guys who would love the color black more than goth's.
  • White Scars: They love speed more than anyone and take it too far sometimes.
  • Black Templars: They will never end their crusade so they can. They also hate everyone who isn't human
  • Iron Hands: They love to amputate them selves as much as a tech priest.

What they have in common

Power armor, big attitudes, and a father who is both alive and not alive at the same time. They argue about tactics but agree that xenos taste better well done.

Chaos Space Marines

Former heroes who took a very wrong turn into the warp. Now they work for angry gods and hate their old coworkers.

Legions and vibes

  • Black Legion: Main force of the chaos space marines who love spikes alot.
  • World Eaters: Walking Geneva Convention violations. Oh and the like axes and blood.
  • Emperors Children: NSFW is there whole thing.
  • Death Guard: Plague walkers who tank everything and smell bad.
  • Thousand Sons: Guys stuck in a suit since someone decided to mess up and make them all sand.
  • Word Bearers: closest thing to a cult asking you to sacrifice yourself.
  • Night Lords: They just love to torture everyone and wear flesh from their victims.
  • Iron Warriors: Siege lovers who find destroying things their life's mission.
  • Alpha Legion: Everything is classified.

What they want

Revenge, worship, and a galaxy size complaint letter to the big e. Also trophies.

Xenos Factions

  • Aeldari: Ancient space elves who read the future and still trip on it.
  • Drukhari: Degenerate Eldar who treat pain like a loyalty program.
  • Harlequins: Eldar theater kids who fight like its a Broad Way show.
  • Orks: Green boys who love nothing more than to fight everyone.
  • Tyranids: Space bugs who just see the galaxy as their lunch.
  • Necrons: Terminators who found out its better to have flesh.
  • Tau: Blue man looking race that only like to fight at range.
  • Leagues of Votann: Space dwarfs that hate the Tyranids.

Who wins

Nobody wins. That is the brand. But it is very fun to watch everyone try.
