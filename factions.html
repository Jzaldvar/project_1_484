Factions of Warhammer 40K

Welcome to the galaxy sized food fight. Below is your fast guide to who is who, why they fight, and what vibe they bring to the party.

Fun fact box

Orks believe red paint makes things go faster. In this setting belief has power, so yes red rides go fast. Science cries. Orks laugh.

The Imperium of Man

The biggest human empire. It runs on faith, paperwork, and a very tired golden skeleton on a chair. If it moves, it needs a stamp. If it breathes, it needs a prayer.

  • Astra Militarum the guard with endless bodies and bigger tanks.
  • Adeptus Mechanicus tech priests who love machines more than friends.
  • Adeptus Custodes the golden bodyguards who never skip leg day.
  • Adepta Sororitas sisters with flamers and faith that melts armor.
  • Inquisition if they ask questions the answer is usually flame.

Loyalist Space Marines

Gene boosted poster boys of humanity. Here are the headliners.

Major Chapters

  • Ultramarines rule book champions. If there is a correct way they wrote it.
  • Blood Angels beautiful warriors with a dramatic thirst problem.
  • Dark Angels very serious knights with secrets stacked on secrets.
  • Space Wolves drink, sing, then jump into battle with teeth and claws.
  • Salamanders kind hearted forge lovers who bring extra flame for your safety.
  • Imperial Fists masters of sieges who enjoy walls more than sunsets.
  • Raven Guard stealth birds who prefer to win before anyone sees them.
  • White Scars speed fanatics who treat bikes like sacred animals.
  • Black Templars eternal crusaders. Every day is holy war day.
  • Iron Hands replace fear with metal. Replace feelings with more metal.

What they have in common

Power armor, big attitudes, and a father who is both alive and not alive at the same time. They argue about tactics but agree that xenos taste better well done.

Chaos Space Marines

Former heroes who took a very wrong turn into the warp. Now they work for angry gods and hate their old coworkers.

Legions and vibes

  • Black Legion the main crew under Abaddon. Big plans and bigger spikes.
  • World Eaters flying axes and louder yelling. Subtle as a meteor.
  • Emperors Children perfection hunters with music that hurts reality.
  • Death Guard patient plague walkers. Rust is a lifestyle choice.
  • Thousand Sons sorcerers with dust in the suits and books in the bag.
  • Word Bearers sermon first, battle second. Dark chaplains for hire.
  • Night Lords terror artists who turn fear into a weapon.
  • Iron Warriors siege lovers who bring more guns than friends.
  • Alpha Legion secret agents of maybe everyone and maybe no one.

What they want

Revenge, worship, and a galaxy size complaint letter to the big e. Also trophies.

Xenos Factions

  • Aeldari ancient space elves who read the future and still trip on it.
  • Drukhari pirates who treat pain like a loyalty program.
  • Harlequins masked dancers who fight like a magic trick.
  • Orks green crowd control. If fun is loud they are the loudest.
  • Tyranids galaxy locusts who turn planets into soup.
  • Necrons metal pharaohs who woke up cranky and want their galaxy back.
  • Tau bright clean armor and friendly talks that end with drones.
  • Leagues of Votann sturdy void travelers who pack compact power.

Who wins

Nobody wins. That is the brand. But it is very fun to watch everyone try.
